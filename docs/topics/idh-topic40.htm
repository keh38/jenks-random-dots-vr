White Noise

 

 

•In this mode, the velocity of each dot is drawn from a 2D Gaussian random distribution

•The mean of the distribution is either zero or equal to -Vchair (see "Add Vchair" option)

•The velocity of each dot is drawn at the beginning of its lifetime. It maintains that velocity throughout the lifetime.

 

 

Parameter

Meaning

Horizontal s.d.

Vertical s.d.

s.d. of the velocity distribution in the horizontal and vertical directions

Units

the s.d. can be expressed either in degrees/s or as a percentage of the chair velocity

Add Vchair

if checked, the mean of the distribution is -Vchair. Mean = 0 otherwise

Identical noise

- all dots are assigned the same (random) velocity

- a new value is drawn at the beginning of each lifetime, but assigned globally

- dots must be strobed!

 

 

 

Note: Horz = Vert = 0 and "Add Vchair" checked → all dots move coherently (same as Brownian motion with Coherence = 1)

 

•The Coherence parameter has no effect in this context
